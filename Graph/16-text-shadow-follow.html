Shadow Follows Light

Move the cursor around the page. Every heading casts its shadow in the opposite direction, as if the pointer were the sun and the text were physical objects.

Pointer As Sunlight

The shadow vector is recalculated for every heading based on the cursor position relative to that element's center.

Opposite Direction Rule

If the mouse moves right, shadows shift left. If the mouse moves down, shadows drift upward.

Layered Depth

Each heading uses multiple soft shadow layers to make the motion feel more graphic and tangible.
